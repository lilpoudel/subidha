Our Story

A local business built for women's health

Subidha Sanitary Udhyog is a home-based manufacturing business located in Suryabinayak-5, Bhaktapur, Nepal. We produce high-quality sanitary pads that are affordable, hygienic, and trusted by families across the region.

Our pads feature Anion Chip technology — a modern innovation that provides anti-fungal, anti-bacterial, and anti-viral protection, giving women the confidence and comfort they deserve.

By buying Subidha, you support a local Nepali business and help us continue providing affordable hygiene products to women in our community.
